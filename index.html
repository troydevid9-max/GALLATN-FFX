Common Questions

FAQ

Gallant FX is a decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol built on the Solana blockchain. GFX is its native utility token used for staking, governance, and earning rewards within the ecosystem. With a fixed supply of 1 billion tokens, GFX is designed for long-term value accrual.
You deposit GFX tokens into our smart contract and choose a lock period — 30, 60, or 90 days. Longer lock periods earn higher reward multipliers up to 22.5% APY. Rewards are calculated automatically and can be claimed at any time. Early unstaking is possible with a penalty fee.
Gallant FX supports all major Solana wallets including Phantom, Solflare, and Backpack. Any wallet that supports SPL tokens can hold GFX. We recommend Phantom for the best experience with the dApp.
Security is our top priority. All smart contracts are undergoing independent third-party audits before mainnet launch. Treasury funds are protected by multi-signature controls requiring multiple keyholders to approve transactions. Audit reports will be publicly available.
GFX tokens can be acquired through our upcoming public sale and will be available to swap on Jupiter Aggregator after our Phase 3 launch. To participate in early access or the public sale, join our community channels and watch for official announcements. Always use the contract address listed on this site.
GFX holders will be able to submit and vote on governance proposals covering protocol parameters, treasury spending, and platform upgrades. Voting power is proportional to staked GFX balance. DAO governance will launch in Phase 4 of the roadmap.
